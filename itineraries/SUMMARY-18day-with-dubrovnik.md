# Croatia & Slovenia 18-Day Itinerary - SUMMARY

## Key Changes from 15-Day to 18-Day Version

### What's New
✅ **Dubrovnik added:** 3 nights (Days 15-17)  
✅ **Total trip:** 18 days (can be done in 17-18 depending on return preference)  
✅ **Rovinj reduced:** 3 nights → 2 nights (still see everything important)  
✅ **All other hubs:** Maintained at optimal length  

### New Itinerary Structure

**Days 1-2:** Travel to Slovenia  
- Day 1: Netherlands → Munich (700 km)
- Day 2: Munich → Lake Bled (400 km)

**Days 3-5:** Lake Bled & Bohinj **(3 nights)** - KEPT ORIGINAL
- Day 3: Lake Bled kayaking, castle, Vintgar Gorge
- Day 4: Lake Bohinj day trip, Savica waterfall
- Day 5: Morning at Bled, drive to Ljubljana

**Days 6-7:** Ljubljana & Postojna **(2 nights)** - KEPT ORIGINAL
- Day 6: Ljubljana city exploration
- Day 7: Postojna Cave & Predjama Castle, drive to Rovinj

**Days 8-9:** Rovinj, Istria **(2 nights)** - REDUCED FROM 3
- Day 8: Rovinj arrival & old town
- Day 9: Sea kayaking OR Pula/Poreč day trip

**Days 10-12:** Šibenik/Zadar + Plitvice **(3 nights)** - KEPT ORIGINAL
- Day 10: Drive via Plitvice Lakes (full day at park)
- Day 11: Krka National Park + Šibenik
- Day 12: Zadar + options, drive to Split

**Days 13-14:** Split + Brač Island **(2 nights)** - KEPT ORIGINAL
- Day 13: Split & Diocletian's Palace
- Day 14: Brač Island - Zlatni Rat beach + Vidova Gora

**Days 15-17:** Dubrovnik **(3 nights)** - ⭐ NEW!
- Day 15: Drive to Dubrovnik (230 km, 3-3.5 hrs), arrival & orientation
- Day 16: City walls walk (8am), Mt. Srđ cable car, Lokrum Island
- Day 17: Sea kayaking around old town, more exploration

**Day 18:** Return Journey **(1-2 days)**
- OPTION A: Drive 800 km to Slovenia/Austria (Day 18), then 950 km home (Day 19)
- OPTION B: One very long day 1,500+ km (only if you're superhuman!)

### Dubrovnik Highlights (3 Days)

**Day 1 (Arrival):**
- Afternoon arrival from Split
- Setup campsite
- Old town orientation walk
- Sunset views
- Dinner in atmospheric setting

**Day 2 (Main Sightseeing):**
- 08:00: City walls walk (2 hours) - beat crowds!
- 10:30: Old town exploration
- 14:00: Cable car to Mt. Srđ (panoramic views)
- Evening: Sunset, dinner

**Day 3 (Water Activities):**
- Morning: Sea kayaking around walls (3-4 hours)
- Afternoon: Lokrum Island ferry (explore island, "Dead Sea" swim, Fort Royal views)
- Evening: Final stroll, pack

**What makes it worth it:**
- City walls walk = one of Europe's best experiences (€35 but worth it)
- Sea kayaking around medieval walls = unforgettable
- Mt. Srđ views = THE iconic Croatian photo
- Lokrum Island = peaceful escape with swimming
- Complete Croatian coast experience

### When to Go: Late September Still Best

**Sept 20-30 perfect for this itinerary because:**
- Dubrovnik crowds manageable (peak over)
- Water still warm (23-25°C)
- Weather excellent (25-28°C days)
- Prices 20-30% lower than peak
- All facilities open
- Photography light spectacular
- No wetsuit needed

**Avoid July-August:** Dubrovnik unbearably crowded (cruise ships dump 6,000+ tourists daily!)
