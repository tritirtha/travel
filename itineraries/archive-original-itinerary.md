# European Campervan Travel Itineraries from Netherlands

**Vehicle:** Peugeot 5008 1.6 THP (2015) towing campervan  
**Starting Point:** Netherlands  
**Travelers:** 2 adults (you and your wife)  
**Activities:** Kayaking (inflatable), hiking, landscape photography, drone videography  
**Driving:** Moderate pace (300-400 km/day)

---

## 10-DAY ITINERARY #1: Norwegian Fjords Adventure
**Best Season:** June-August  
**Total Distance:** ~3,200 km  
**Highlights:** Dramatic fjords, waterfalls, mountain kayaking, midnight sun (summer)

### Day 1: Netherlands → Northern Germany
- **Drive:** 450 km to Hamburg area
- **Overnight:** Near Lübeck
- **Activities:** Easy first day, settle into campervan life
- **Photography:** Historic Hanseatic towns

### Day 2: Germany → Denmark
- **Drive:** 400 km through Denmark
- **Overnight:** Near Aalborg or continue to Hirtshals
- **Activities:** Coastal views along the way
- **Tip:** Stock up on groceries in Germany (cheaper than Norway)

### Day 3: Ferry to Norway → Bergen Area
- **Ferry:** Hirtshals to Kristiansand or Stavanger (book in advance!)
- **Drive:** 200-300 km to Bergen region
- **Overnight:** Near Bergen or Hardangerfjord
- **Activities:** Evening exploration of Bergen if time allows
- **Drone:** Bergen's colorful Bryggen wharf (check local regulations)

### Day 4: Hardangerfjord
- **Drive:** Minimal (explore locally)
- **Kayaking:** Hardangerfjord - calm waters, fruit orchards along shores
- **Hiking:** Trolltunga if ambitious (10-12 hours) or shorter trails
- **Photography:** Waterfalls (Vøringsfossen), fruit blossoms (spring/summer)
- **Drone:** Epic fjord shots from various viewpoints

### Day 5: Sognefjord - Norway's Longest Fjord
- **Drive:** 200 km via scenic routes
- **Kayaking:** Nærøyfjord (UNESCO site) - narrow dramatic fjord
- **Hiking:** Stegastein viewpoint or easier trails
- **Photography:** Flåm railway valley, steep mountain walls
- **Overnight:** Flåm or Aurland area

### Day 6: Geirangerfjord
- **Drive:** 300 km via mountain passes
- **Kayaking:** Geirangerfjord - most photogenic fjord
- **Activities:** Seven Sisters waterfall, abandoned farms on cliffs
- **Photography/Drone:** Dalsnibba viewpoint (1,500m), endless epic shots
- **Overnight:** Geiranger village

### Day 7: Atlantic Road
- **Drive:** 350 km
- **Activities:** Atlantic Road bridges (iconic photo location)
- **Photography:** Dramatic coastal architecture, ocean views
- **Overnight:** Kristiansund or Molde area
- **Note:** Start heading south

### Day 8: Return Journey South
- **Drive:** 400 km toward Southern Norway
- **Route:** Through mountain passes, lakes
- **Overnight:** Lillehammer or similar
- **Activities:** Relaxed driving day with photo stops

### Day 9: Southern Norway → Denmark Ferry
- **Drive:** 350 km to Kristiansand
- **Ferry:** Evening ferry to Hirtshals
- **Overnight:** On ferry or Denmark

### Day 10: Denmark → Netherlands
- **Drive:** 500 km home
- **Activities:** Final grocery shopping, rest stops
- **Arrive:** Evening at home

**Budget Estimate:**
- Fuel: €500-600 (Norway is expensive)
- Ferry: €300-500 (varies by season, campervan size)
- Camping: €300-400 (Norway allows wild camping in many areas)
- Food/Activities: €400-600
- **Total:** €1,500-2,100

**Drone Notes:** Norway has liberal drone rules in remote areas, but restricted near airports and Bergen city center. Perfect for landscape shots.

## 10-DAY ITINERARY #2: Alpine Lakes Circuit
**Best Season:** June-September  
**Total Distance:** ~2,800 km  
**Highlights:** Swiss/Italian/Austrian lakes, mountain scenery, turquoise waters

### Day 1: Netherlands → Rhine Valley, Germany
- **Drive:** 400 km
- **Overnight:** Rhine Valley area (Bacharach/Boppard)
- **Activities:** Rhine castles photography
- **Photography:** Sunset over castle-dotted hillsides

### Day 2: Germany → Lake Constance (Bodensee)
- **Drive:** 350 km through Black Forest
- **Overnight:** Lake Constance (Konstanz area)
- **Kayaking:** Lake Constance - calm, beautiful setting
- **Activities:** Island of Mainau (flower island)
- **Photography:** Alps appearing across the lake

### Day 3: Switzerland → Interlaken Region
- **Drive:** 300 km
- **Overnight:** Interlaken or Lauterbrunnen
- **Activities:** Explore Lauterbrunnen valley (72 waterfalls)
- **Photography:** Staubbach Falls, Swiss chalets
- **Note:** Switzerland is expensive; stock up before border

### Day 4: Lakes Thun & Brienz
- **Drive:** Minimal local exploration
- **Kayaking:** Lake Brienz (turquoise glacial water) - stunning!
- **Hiking:** Easy trails around lakes or cable car to Harder Kulm
- **Photography/Drone:** Iseltwald pier, mountain reflections
- **Activities:** Jungfraujoch optional (expensive but spectacular)

### Day 5: Italy → Lake Como
- **Drive:** 350 km via mountain passes (St. Gotthard/Simplon)
- **Overnight:** Varenna or Bellano
- **Activities:** Evening lakeside stroll, Italian gelato
- **Photography:** Villages clinging to mountainsides
- **Drone:** Como's dramatic topography (check Italian regulations)

### Day 6: Lake Como Day
- **Drive:** Minimal
- **Kayaking:** Lake Como - explore hidden villas and coves
- **Activities:** Visit Bellagio, villa gardens
- **Photography:** Sunrise/sunset over lake, colorful villages
- **Evening:** Italian dinner lakeside

### Day 7: Italy → Lago di Garda
- **Drive:** 200 km
- **Overnight:** Riva del Garda (northern end)
- **Kayaking:** Lake Garda - great winds and waves (check conditions)
- **Activities:** Medieval castle in Riva

### Day 8: Austria → Achensee
- **Drive:** 250 km through Dolomites (optional detour)
- **Overnight:** Achensee, Austria
- **Kayaking:** Achensee - Austria's largest alpine lake

### Day 9: Austria → Bavaria → Germany
- **Drive:** 350 km
- **Route:** Through Bavarian Alps, past Neuschwanstein Castle
- **Overnight:** Munich area or continue north
- **Activities:** Castle photography if time allows
- **Photography:** Fairy-tale castles

### Day 10: Germany → Netherlands
- **Drive:** 400 km home
- **Activities:** Relaxed return journey

**Budget Estimate:**
- Fuel: €450-550
- Tolls: €150-200 (Swiss vignette, Italian tolls)
- Camping: €400-500 (Alps are pricier)
- Food/Activities: €500-700 (Switzerland expensive)
- **Total:** €1,500-1,950

**Drone Notes:** Switzerland and Italy have restrictions near populated areas. Best shots at lakes early morning with fewer people.

## PRACTICAL TIPS FOR ALL TRIPS

### Towing with Peugeot 5008 1.6 THP (2015)
- **Max Towing Capacity:** Check your specific model (typically 1,300-1,650 kg)
- **Driving Tips:**
  - Allow 30-50% more time for journeys
  - Mountain passes will be slow - be patient
  - Fuel consumption will increase significantly (40-60%)
  - Check tire pressure regularly (car and campervan)
  - Use lower gears on descents
  - Practice reversing with trailer before the trip!

### Campervan & Camping
- **Apps:** Park4Night, Campercard, iOverlander (find camping spots)
- **Wild Camping Legal:**
  - Norway, Sweden, Scotland (with restrictions)
  - Tolerated: Many parts of France, Spain (away from towns)
  - Prohibited: Most of Switzerland, Italy beaches, German cities
- **Facilities:** Plan for dump stations, water refills

### Photography & Drone Equipment
- **Best Light:** Golden hour (sunrise/sunset)
- **Drone Registration:**
  - EU: Register in one country, valid in all EU
  - UK: Register with CAA if >250g
  - Norway: Separate registration
- **Drone Insurance:** Get it! Mandatory in many countries
- **Weather App:** Windy.com (excellent for planning drone flights)
- **Backup:** Bring extra batteries, memory cards

### Kayaking Safety
- **Inflatable Quality:** Ensure it's rated for your activities
- **Always Wear:** Life jackets
- **Weather:** Check forecasts and water conditions
- **Wildlife:** Respect seals, birds (nesting seasons)
- **Storage:** Dry thoroughly to prevent mold in campervan

### Travel Documents & Essentials
- **Documents:**
  - Passports
  - International Driving Permit (for non-EU countries like UK post-Brexit)
  - Vehicle registration
  - Insurance (including trailer)
  - European Health Insurance Card (EHIC)
  - Breakdown cover (ANWB or similar)
  
- **Required in Vehicle:**
  - Warning triangles
  - Reflective vests
  - First aid kit
  - Headlamp beam deflectors
  - GB/NL sticker
  
- **Vignettes Needed:**
  - Switzerland: Annual (CHF 40)
  - Austria: 10-day (€9.90)
  - Slovenia: Weekly (€15)

### Money Matters
- **Currencies:**
  - Eurozone: Most countries
  - Non-Euro: UK (GBP), Norway (NOK), Switzerland (CHF)
- **Tips:** Many places prefer card payments now
- **Budget Apps:** Splitwise, Trail Wallet

### Best Photography Locations Summary
1. **Norway:** Geirangerfjord, Trolltunga, Atlantic Road
2. **Scotland:** Isle of Skye, Glencoe, Eilean Donan Castle
3. **Alps:** Lake Bled, Lake Brienz, Lauterbrunnen
4. **Croatia:** Plitvice Lakes, Dubrovnik walls, Rovinj
5. **France:** Dune du Pilat, Gavarnie Cirque, Loire castles

### Mobile Connectivity
- **EU Roaming:** Free in EU (check your plan)
- **UK:** May have charges post-Brexit
- **Norway/Switzerland:** Not EU - check rates or get local SIM

### Food & Cooking
- **Grocery Shopping:**
  - Cheapest: Lidl, Aldi (everywhere)
  - Stock up before expensive countries (Norway, Switzerland)
  - Try local markets for fresh produce
- **Campervan Cooking:** Plan simple meals, Dutch oven recipes
- **Dining Out:** Budget for local specialties (Norwegian fish, Italian pasta, Spanish pintxos)

### Seasonal Considerations
- **Spring (March-May):** Fewer crowds, flowers blooming, some mountain passes closed
- **Summer (June-August):** Best weather, crowded, expensive, all roads open
- **Fall (September-October):** Beautiful colors, moderate weather, good deals
- **Winter:** Only for experienced; many areas closed/challenging
