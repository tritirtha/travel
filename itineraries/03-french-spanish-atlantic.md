# French & Spanish Atlantic + Pyrenees - 15 Days

**Best Season:** May-June or September-October (Avoid peak summer heat)  
**Total Campervan Distance:** ~4,800 km  
**Driving Style:** Long initial/return drives (600-700 km), hub stays (2-3 nights)  
**Campsites:** Atlantic coast and mountain bases with car day trips

---

## Overview

Experience France and Spain's dramatic Atlantic coastline combined with the spectacular Pyrenees mountains. From Europe's tallest sand dune to Basque culture, from vineyard landscapes to alpine peaks. Perfect for varied photography, coastal and mountain kayaking, and cultural immersion.

## CAMPSITE HUB #1: Arcachon Bay Area
**Location:** Arcachon or Pyla-sur-Mer  
**Nights:** 3 nights (Days 3-5)  
**Move:** 200 km from La Rochelle

### Day 3: Travel + Dune du Pilat
**Campervan Drive:** 200 km (2.5-3 hours)

#### Afternoon: Dune du Pilat
- **Arrive:** Early afternoon
- **Europe's tallest sand dune:** 110m high, 2.7 km long
- **Climb:** Stairs or sandy slope
- **Views:**
  - Atlantic Ocean on one side
  - Pine forest (Landes) on other
  - Bay of Arcachon
- **Photography/Drone:**
  - Incredible aerial shots
  - Sunset golden light on sand
  - Forest meeting ocean
  - Paragliders often present
- **Duration:** 2-3 hours

#### Evening
- Setup campsite
- Arcachon town walk
- Famous for oysters!

#### Campsite Options
- **Camping Panorama du Pyla:** Near dune
- **Arcachon area campsites:** Multiple options
- €35-45/night

### Day 4: Arcachon Bay Exploration (Day Trip)
**Car Round Trip:** ~60 km

#### Morning: Kayaking Arcachon Bay
- **Launch:** Multiple points around bay
- **Explore:**
  - Île aux Oiseaux (Bird Island)
  - Cabanes Tchanquées (stilt houses) - iconic!
  - Oyster farming areas
  - Banc d'Arguin (sandbank nature reserve)
- **Conditions:** Protected bay, very calm
- **Duration:** 3-4 hours
- **Rental widely available:** €15-25

#### Lunch
- **Fresh oysters at oyster shacks:**
  - Cap Ferret area
  - Served with bread, wine
  - Affordable and delicious
  - Quintessential local experience

#### Afternoon Options

**Option A: Cap Ferret Peninsula**
- **Drive:** Narrow peninsula separating bay from ocean
- **Lighthouse:** Climb for 360° views
- **Beaches:** Both ocean and bay sides
- **Villages:** Charming oyster villages
- **Bike-friendly:** Rental available

**Option B: Arcachon Town**
- **Winter Town (Ville d'Hiver):**
  - 19th-century villas
  - Belle Époque architecture
  - Panoramic lift
- **Beach promenade**
- **Relaxed afternoon**

#### Photography
- Oyster beds at low tide
- Stilt houses
- Dune from water
- Sunset over bay

### Day 5: Bordeaux Wine Region (Day Trip by Car)
**Car Round Trip:** ~150 km

#### Morning: Drive to Bordeaux Vineyards
- **Region options:**
  - **Médoc:** North of Bordeaux (famous châteaux)
  - **Saint-Émilion:** East of Bordeaux (UNESCO medieval village)
  - **Graves:** South of Bordeaux

- **Cost:** €10-50 per tasting

#### Saint-Émilion (Recommended)
- **Medieval hilltop village**
- **Monolithic church** carved from rock
- **Rampart walk** with views
- **Many wine shops** for tasting
- **Lunch:** In village square
- **2-3 hours visit**

#### Photography
- Vineyard landscapes
- Château architecture
- Rolling hills of vines
- Medieval village

#### Alternative
- **Bordeaux City:**
  - Place de la Bourse (mirror fountain)
  - Historic center (UNESCO)
  - Cité du Vin (wine museum)

### Day 7: San Sebastián & Basque Coast (Day Trip)
**Car Round Trip:** ~50 km

  - One of Europe's best city beaches
  - Crescent-shaped bay
  - Swimming/relaxing

- **Monte Igueldo:**
  - Funicular to summit
  - 360° views of city and coast

- **Old Town (Parte Vieja):**
  - Narrow streets
  - Pintxo bars everywhere
  - La Bretxa market

#### Lunch: Pintxos Experience
- **Basque tapas** (pintxos/pinchos)
- **Bar hopping:** Traditional way to eat
- **Top picks:** Calle 31 de Agosto
- **Cost:** €2-4 per pintxo + drink
- **Try:** Gilda, tortilla, txangurro (spider crab)

**Option A: Coastal Kayaking**
- **Launch:** La Concha or nearby beaches

- **Suitable for:** Experienced kayakers

**Option B: Monte Urgull**
- **Hike:** Through park to summit (30 min)
- **Castillo de la Mota:** Fortress ruins
- **Views:** City and bay

**Option C: Aquarium**
- **Excellent aquarium:** Underwater tunnel
- **Oceanographic museum**

- Pintxo culture

### Day 10: Cirque de Gavarnie (Day Trip by Car)
**Car Round Trip:** ~40 km + hiking

#### Cirque de Gavarnie (UNESCO)
- **Massive natural amphitheater:** 3,000m walls
- **Grande Cascade:** 422m waterfall (Europe's highest)
  
**Hike:**
- **Easy walk:** 1.5 hours each way from village
- **Mostly flat:** Suitable for all levels
- **Spectacular:** Increasingly dramatic views
- **Horses available:** For those who prefer (€20-30)

**Photography:**
- Dramatic mountain cirque
- Waterfall against massive cliffs
- Snow-capped peaks (year-round snow)
- Wildflowers (spring/summer)

#### Afternoon Options
- **Longer hike:** Continue to cirque wall
- **Drive:** Scenic mountain roads
- **Port de Boucharo viewpoint**
- **Village exploration**

### Day 11: Pont d'Espagne & Mountain Lakes (Day Trip)
**Car Round Trip:** ~30 km + hiking

#### Morning: Pont d'Espagne
- **Drive:** From Cauterets (15 km)
- **Parking:** At Pont d'Espagne (€6)
- **Bridge:** Spans rushing mountain stream
- **Waterfall views**

#### Hiking Options

**Option A: Lac de Gaube** (Recommended)
- **Hike:** 1 hour up (moderate)
- **Or Chairlift:** €10 one-way, €15 return
- **Lake:** Glacial turquoise color
- **Vignemale:** Peak reflected in lake (3,298m)
- **Kayaking:** Can carry inflatable kayak up!
  - Very cold water
  - Stunning alpine paddling
  - Unique experience

**Option B: Longer Hikes**
- **Lac d'Estom:** Continue beyond Gaube (4 hours total)
- **GR10 trail sections:** Long-distance path
- **Multiple waterfalls**

#### Photography/Drone
- Alpine lakes
- Mountain reflections
- Waterfalls
- Pyrenees peaks
- Marmots (common here!)

#### Afternoon
- Return to campsite
- Relax after mountain days

## Practical Tips

### Driving France/Spain
- **French autoroutes (tolls):**
  - Pay at booths
  - Credit cards accepted
  - Can be expensive (budget €100-150)
  
- **Spanish autopistas:**
  - Some toll, some free
  - Generally less expensive than France

- **Mountain roads:**
  - Narrow, winding
  - Take it slow
  - Passing places

### Kayaking Specifics
- **Atlantic coast:**
  - Can have waves/swells
  - Check conditions before launching
  - Bays/protected areas safer
  
- **Arcachon Bay:** Perfect for beginners (calm)
- **Basque coast:** More challenging (experienced)
- **Mountain lakes:** Cold! But stunning

### Weather
- **Coast:** Mild, can be windy
- **Basque Country:** Rainy (bring rain gear!)
- **Pyrenees:** Variable mountain weather
  - Can change rapidly
  - Cooler at altitude
  - Check forecasts

### Languages
- **French:** Main language
- **Spanish:** In Spain (obviously!)
- **Basque (Euskara):** Bilingual signage in Basque Country
- **English:** Varies (better in tourist areas)

### Food Culture
- **France:**
  - Formal dining traditions
  - Excellent bread, cheese, wine
  - Markets for fresh produce
  
- **Basque Country:**
  - Pintxo bar culture
  - Seafood focus
  - Communal dining atmosphere
  - Cider houses (sagardotegi)

### Photography & Drone
- **Drone regulations:**
  - **France:** Register drones >250g
  - **Spain:** Similar rules
  - Restricted near airports, military
  - National parks may have rules
  
- **Best shots:**
  - Dune du Pilat from above
  - Oyster beds and stilt houses
  - Basque coastline
  - Pyrenees peaks and lakes
  - Vineyard landscapes
